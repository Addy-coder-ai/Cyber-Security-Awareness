CYBER SECURITY AWARENESS

Introduction About Tips and tricks




What is Cyber security awareness?

Cyber awareness refers to the level of awareness and understanding that users have about cyber security, the cyber threats that their networks or organizations face everyday and the ways to tackle those threats.


Why should we be aware of cyber security?

Cyber threats are getting increasingly common nowadays and hackers can easily get your personal data and bank information.

There are many diferent kinds of threats like:

  • Phishing
  • Malware
  • Distributed Denial of service(DDoS) attack
  • Spoofing

Some Major tips for keeping your computer safe:


  • Keep software up-to-date
  • Avoid opening suspicious emails
  • Keep hardware up-to-date
  • Use a secure file-sharing solution to encrypt data
  • Use anti-virus and anti-malware
  • Use a VPN to privatize your connections
  • Check links before you click
  • Disable Bluetooth when you don't need it
  • Enable 2-Factor Authentication
  • Remove adware from your machines
  • Double-check for HTTPS on websites
  • Avoid using public networks
  • Invest in security upgrades
  • Back up important data
  • Use HTTPS on your website
